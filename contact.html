Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer service hours? +

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9am to 6pm IST, and Saturdays from 10am to 4pm IST. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What is your return policy? +

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return. Some exclusions may apply.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working to expand our shipping options in the future. Please check back or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated.

How do I care for my Outfique products? +

Care instructions vary by product. Please refer to the care label included with your purchase or visit the product page on our website for specific care instructions.
